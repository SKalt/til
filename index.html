Common Platform Enumeration, a syntax for identifying “IT stuff”:

cpe
:<cpe_version>
:<part>
:<vendor>
:<product>
:<version>
:<update>
:<edition>
:<language>
:<sw_edition>
:<target_sw>
:<target_hw>
:<other>

Notably, part can only be a|h|o = app | hardware | OS, not “documentation”.

See https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Common_Platform_Enumeration
