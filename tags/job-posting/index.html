The meaning of the tags

  • #LI-Remote: Remote
  • #LI-Hybrid: Hybrid
  • #LI-Onsite: Onsite
  • #LI-DNI: To prevent jobs from displaying on LinkedIn completely. (DNI = Do Not Ingest)
  • #LI-DNP: To prevent jobs from filling a job slot, use this tag or use the ‘block job’ functionality.
