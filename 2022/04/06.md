That python f-strings can do 
- date and time formatting: `f"{today:%Y-%m-%d}"`
- have a variable-debugging format: `f"{x = }, {y = }"`
- can use `__repr__` rather than `__str__`: `f"{user!r}"`
- can be nested: `f"output: {value:{width}.{precision}}"`
