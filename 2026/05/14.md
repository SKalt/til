> in e.g. .NET, python, and rust, the default `\d` matches Unicode digits (~370 digits in .NET, ~770 in Rust), not just ASCII 0-9. that includes Eastern Arabic (٠١٢٣), Devanagari (०१२३), Fullwidth (０１２３), and many others.
